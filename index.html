Thrissur Pooram is a magnificent Kerala festival renowned for its grand procession of decorated elephants, vibrant fireworks, and traditional percussion music, showcasing the region's rich cultural heritage.

ONAM

Onam is a vibrant and joyous festival celebrated in Kerala, marked by intricate flower arrangements, traditional dances, and a grand feast known as Onam Sadhya, reflecting the region's cultural richness and community spirit.

PULI KALI

Puli Kali is a lively Onam celebration in Kerala, featuring performers painted as tigers who dance to energetic beats, adding a colorful and dynamic spectacle to the festival's festivities.
